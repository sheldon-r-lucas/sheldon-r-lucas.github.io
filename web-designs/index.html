KNOWLEDGE BASE

INTRODUCING RESPONSIVE WEBSITE DESIGN

Responsive websites are those websites that adjust themselves according to the dimension of device viewing it. For example, if you will open SanovaTEC on your mobile phone, I-pod or Tablet PC, you will see that the website adjusts itself so that viewer can browse it effortlessly.

According to recent surveys in the USA, around 67% net users uses their I-phone to access the Internet. Even in India, it is estimated that there will be more than 350 Million users by the end of 2017, of which 50% use mobile phones to access the Internet. Around 70% of young net users  depend primarily on mobile access to the Internet.

If your website does not display correctly on all devices, then it can negatively affect your business.

 

SanovaTEC build or re-build your website with such easy browsing solutions to cater to the next generation of mobile devices users.

Whether you are selling a product, serving a customer, or improving efficiency, SanovaTEC Corporation designs websites that meet your business requirements and caters to your customer’s needs.
