Meet our leadership Team

Mr. Michael Bowen
Director Operations
Mr. Bowen is a goal-oriented individual with strong leadership capabilities and strong background in people management and development. He is an organized, highly motivated, and detail-directed problem solver with excellent skills set in Microsoft Office and Adobe Creative Suite. Possessing a strong training background, he served as an instructor for a Quality Investment Program for youth to equip participants with business management skills including Budgeting and Forecasting, Manpower Planning, Cost and Inventory Control, and Cost Accounting. He has served as a specialist instructor, facilitator and presenter in employee training programs such as Effective Presentation Skills, Financial Management for the Non-Financial Manager, Effective Leadership Improving Habits, and Attitudes and Work Ethics. He has been a tutor in Computer Information Technology Training for the Ministry Social Transformation’s for over sixteen (16) years. Michael is skilled in performing ongoing schedule analyses. He has implemented findings to increase efficiencies and decrease customer service delays. He has also conceptualized, designed and implemented the Production Control department for the largest claims processing company in Barbados.
Ms. Sonia Sobers MSc, MCSE, CCNA, CEH, MVMP, ATMP
Director Information Security and Forensic Services
Ms. Sonia Sobers is a dedicated, computer security forensics specialist, who throughout her working career as an IT support professional, has gained deep knowledge of a cross-section of industries, including Accounting, Factory Production, Commercial Banking, Hospitality, Telecommunication, Education and Government. As a Microsoft partner since 1999, Ms. Sobers brings over seventeen (17) years of experience in designing, implementing, configuring, installing, administering and supporting Microsoft Enterprise Domains to industry specifications in Barbados and the Eastern Caribbean. She believes that the combination of experience and qualifications allows her to add value to SanovaTECteam as they seek to provide total network security solutions to benefit their clientele. Ms. Sobers holds a Master of Science degree in Computer Forensics and Security, a management of Information Technology certification from the Barbados Institute of Management and Productivity – BIMAP. She is also a Certified Ethical Hacker – CEH,a Certified Cisco Network Administrator – CCNA, a Qualified Diebold Automatic Teller Machine - ATM and a NRT Mobile Vending Machine - MVM professional.
Mr. Charles Walton, CISA, CISM, ABCP, MCSE
Director – Business Continuity Services
Mr. Charles Walton is a Certified Information Technology and Security professional of twenty (20) years’ experience with strong technical, managerial and leadership skill sets developed in various professional support, management and civic leadership roles. His core competencies are in strategic planning, operational management, ICT, Information Security management, and assurance, Network Management, Disaster Recovery and Business Continuity planning. Mr. Walton has functioned in a number of roles from support engineer to manager for a myriad of clients in the financial, tourism, property management, international business and general commercial industries on a range of solutions. He has also had responsibility for the information security management of a large organisation with offices worldwide. He has led several ICT projects including Barbados national tourism website development, voice and data network installation, disaster recovery and business continuity planning, and general enterprise application solutions such accounting and human resource information management from the needs analysis through Request For Proposal - RFP development, tender evaluation, vendor selection to implementation. He is a Certified Information Security Auditor – CISA; Certified Information Security Manager – CISM, Associate Business Continuity Professional– ABCP and a Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer – MCSE.
Ms. Ray Baptiste, MBA, BSc
Director - Projects, Sales and Marketing
Ms. Baptiste has held Lead Project Management and Oversight responsibility for several projects both locally and regionally. Her core competencies include strategic planning, project and process management, business development and enterprise solution sales As a motivated and versatile Telecommunications and IT Professional combining experience with professional certification and training, she adds great value, ensuring that project delivery meets stated objectives. She brings with her sound technical knowledge and over twenty (20) years of deep experience working with regional governments, small and mid-sized businesses firms and Enterprises. She has implemented a broad spectrum of solutions from banking technology upgrades, credit card solution implementation, regional police services infrastructure implementation, education sector ICT deployment and many more. Her attention to detail helps ensure that quality and consistency form a significant part of the customer experience.
